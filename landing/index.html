Frequently Asked

Your questions, answered

Everything you need to know before starting your project.

How much does a bathroom renovation cost?
Project costs depend on size, materials, and scope. A tub-to-shower conversion typically starts around $8,000, while a full remodel ranges from $15,000 to $40,000+. We offer transparent, no-obligation estimates and will never pressure you into a decision.
How long does installation take?
Most tub-to-shower conversions and bath replacements are completed in 1 to 2 days. Full remodels typically take 3 to 7 days depending on scope. We'll give you a clear timeline upfront so you can plan around it.
What does your lifetime warranty cover?
Our lifetime warranty covers all installation workmanship and the core materials we use (acrylic surrounds, shower bases, and plumbing connections) for as long as you own your home. If something ever fails due to our install, we come back and fix it at no charge.
Do you offer financing?
Yes. We work with several trusted lenders to offer flexible financing plans, including 0% interest options for qualified homeowners. Your design specialist will walk you through available plans during your consultation.
Do I need to be home during the installation?
You don't need to supervise, but we do recommend being home at the start and end of each day so we can walk you through progress and address any questions. Our installers are vetted, background-checked, and fully insured.
What areas do you serve?
We serve the entire Greater Toronto Area and beyond — including Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Oakville, Burlington, Hamilton, and surrounding communities. Take the quiz and enter your postal code to confirm coverage.
